# Prompt 020

## Category
Logical & Ethical Reasoning

## Prompt

Explain the potential benefits and risks of using AI for medical diagnostic imaging.

## Response A

Using AI in diagnostic imaging has key pros and cons:

**Benefits:**
- **Increased Accuracy**: AI can detect micro-nodules or fractures that human eyes might miss.
- **Workflow Efficiency**: Triages cases so radiologists see urgent cases first.

**Risks:**
- **Algorithmic Bias**: Models trained on specific demographics may perform poorly on others.
- **Explainability (Black Box)**: Hard to trace how the AI arrived at a specific diagnosis, creating liability concerns.

## Response B

AI implementation in medical imaging presents several facets:

**Advantages:**
- **Speed & Efficiency**: Quickly scans thousands of images, reducing diagnostic backlogs.
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identifies early-stage tumors and anomalies with high consistency.

**Disadvantages:**
- **Lack of Interpretability**: Radiologists cannot easily inspect the model's logic.
- **Data Generalization**: A model trained in one hospital might fail in another due to scanner calibration differences.

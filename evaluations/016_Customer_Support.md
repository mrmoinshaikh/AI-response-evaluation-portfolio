# Prompt 016

## Category
Professional Writing

## Prompt

Reply to a customer whose ordered glass vase arrived shattered. Offer a refund or replacement.

## Response A

Dear Customer,

I am so sorry to hear that your glass vase arrived shattered! We package our items carefully, and it is disappointing when they don't arrive in perfect condition.

We would be happy to resolve this for you. Please let us know if you would prefer:
1. A full refund to your original payment method.
2. A free replacement shipped immediately.

Please reply with a photo of the damaged vase so we can update our shipping team.

Best regards,
Customer Support

## Response B

Dear Customer,

We are deeply sorry to hear that your glass vase arrived broken. We want to make this right. 

Please reply to this email letting us know whether you would prefer a full refund or a replacement vase sent out to you at no extra charge. If possible, please attach a photo of the damage so we can file a claim with the carrier.

Thank you for your patience.

Warmly,
Support Team

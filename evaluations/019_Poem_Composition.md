# Prompt 019

## Category
Creative Writing

## Prompt

Write a 2-stanza poem with an AABB rhyme scheme about the changing of the seasons.

## Response A

The summer warmth begins to fade,
As golden leaves fall in the glade.
The chilly wind begins to blow,
Predicting winter's silent snow.

But soon the spring will wake the earth,
And give the sleeping flowers birth.
The sun will shine so bright and high,
Underneath a clear blue sky.

## Response B

The leaves are falling to the ground, (A)
The summer days are far away. (B)
The cold wind makes a quiet sound, (A)
And winter snow is here to stay. (B)

The flowers start to bloom in spring, (C)
The sun is shining warm and bright. (D)
As little birds begin to sing, (C)
Replacing winter's longest night. (D)

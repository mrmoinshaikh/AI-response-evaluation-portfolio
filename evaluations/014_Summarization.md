## Category
Information Processing

## Prompt

Summarize photosynthesis in exactly 3 bullet points.

## Response A

- **Light Absorption**: Chlorophyll in plant leaves absorbs sunlight to capture energy.
- **Water Splitting**: This energy splits water molecules, releasing oxygen as a byproduct.
- **Glucose Synthesis**: Carbon dioxide is combined with hydrogen to produce glucose (food for the plant).

## Response B

- **Sunlight Capture**: Chlorophyll absorbs solar energy inside chloroplasts.
- **Water Consumption**: Plants absorb water from roots and split it to release oxygen.
- **Carbon Dioxide Intake**: Carbon dioxide is captured from the air.
- **Sugar Creation**: Chemical energy converts CO2 into glucose.

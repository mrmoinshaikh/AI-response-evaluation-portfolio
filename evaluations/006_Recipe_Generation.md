## Prompt

Create a healthy recipe using only chicken, sweet potato, spinach, and standard pantry spices.

## Response A

### Spiced Chicken and Sweet Potato Sheet Pan Dinner

**Ingredients:**
- Chicken breast (cubed)
- Sweet potato (cubed)
- Fresh spinach
- Pantry Spices: Olive oil, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika

**Instructions:**
1. Toss cubed sweet potato and chicken with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
2. Spread on a baking sheet and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 25 minutes.
3. Remove from oven, toss in fresh spinach, and let it wilt in the residual heat.

## Response B

### Skillet Chicken, Sweet Potato, & Spinach Hash

**Ingredients:**
- Chicken breast (diced)
- Sweet potato (grated)
- Fresh spinach
- Red bell pepper (diced)
- Onion (diced)
- Pantry Spices: Butter, salt, black pepper, cumin

**Instructions:**
1. Melt butter in a skillet. Sauté diced onions and bell peppers.
2. Add sweet potato and chicken, cooking until cooked through.
3. Stir in spinach at the end until wilted, seasoning with salt, pepper, and cumin.

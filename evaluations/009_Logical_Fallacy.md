# Prompt 009

## Category
Logical & Ethical Reasoning

## Prompt

Identify the fallacy: 'If we let kids use calculators, they will never learn math and our engineering will collapse.'

## Response A

The fallacy in the statement is the **Slippery Slope** fallacy. This fallacy occurs when an argument claims that a relatively small first step will inevitably lead to a chain of negative events, without providing any logical proof or mechanism linking these steps together. In this case, using calculators is assumed to lead to the complete collapse of engineering, which is an extreme and unsupported link.

## Response B

This statement represents a **Straw Man** fallacy. A straw man fallacy happens when someone exaggerates or misrepresents an argument to make it easier to attack. By claiming that calculators will destroy engineering, the speaker is attacking an exaggerated version of the educational use of technology.

# Prompt 005

## Category
Professional Writing

## Prompt

Draft a professional resignation email giving a 2-week notice and offering transition help.

## Response A

Subject: Resignation - [Your Name]

Dear [Manager's Name],

Please accept this email as formal notification that I am resigning from my position as [Job Title]. My last day will be [Last Day Date, 2 weeks from today].

I want to thank you for the opportunities I've had during my time here. I appreciate your guidance and support.

I am committed to making this transition as smooth as possible. I will complete my outstanding projects and am happy to assist in training other team members before my departure.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

## Response B

Subject: Moving Forward - Resignation of [Your Name]

Dear [Manager's Name],

It is with a heavy heart that I write this email to let you know I will be leaving my role as [Job Title]. I am giving my official two-week notice, meaning my last day will be [Date].

Working here has been an incredible journey. I still remember my first day and all the memories we shared during the late-night sprints. I want to express my deepest gratitude for all the conversations we've had, which really helped me grow.

I want to help with the transition. I can draft documentation and sit down with anyone to train them. I hope we stay in touch in the future.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
